Cut 3D

"Cut 3D" is an innovative puzzle game that challenges your creativity and precision. Get ready to slice through various 3D shapes and objects to achieve the perfect cuts and solve intricate puzzles.

Description  Cut 3D

  • Engaging Puzzles: Tackle a variety of engaging puzzles that require you to cut 3D objects into specified shapes. Each level presents a new challenge that tests your spatial awareness and problem-solving skills.
  • Precision Cutting: Use precise cutting tools to slice through 3D shapes with accuracy. The more precise your cuts, the higher your score and the more rewards you earn.
  • Customizable Tools: Choose from a range of cutting tools, each with unique features. Upgrade your tools to enhance their precision and efficiency, making it easier to complete challenging levels.
  • Realistic Physics: Experience realistic physics as you cut through various materials. Each material reacts differently to your cuts, adding a layer of complexity and realism to the game.
  • Multiple Game Modes: Enjoy different game modes including timed challenges, puzzle solving, and free play. Compete against yourself or other players to achieve the best cuts and highest scores.

How to Play:

  • Select Tool: Choose your preferred cutting tool from the available options. Each tool has unique characteristics, so select one that matches the requirements of the puzzle.
  • Customize Tool: Customize your tool by upgrading its performance and appearance. Use earned rewards to enhance the tool’s cutting precision, speed, and style.
  • Choose Puzzle: Select a puzzle to solve from the puzzle selection menu. Start with easier puzzles to practice your cutting skills before moving on to more complex challenges.
  • Cut: Use the mouse or touchscreen to control your cutting tool. Slice through the 3D object by dragging your tool across the screen. Aim for precise cuts to match the specified shape and complete the puzzle.
  • Complete Objectives: Complete objectives such as achieving a target shape, finishing within a certain time limit, or making a specific number of cuts to earn rewards and unlock new puzzles and tools.
  • Compete: Compete against AI or challenge other players in multiplayer mode. Show off your cutting skills and aim to reach the top of the leaderboard.
